Who we are

Telekom Equator is a telecommunications company that offers a wide range of ICT services. We are comitted to serve and empower Uganda's rural communities through the facilitation of mobile and computer use in the community.

As the pioneers of internet cafes and rural outreach programs in Eastern Uganda, we continue to serve the region and expand our offers through our company.

Our range is compiled with the greatest possible care and personal service, just as we would like it ourselves. We look forward to your visit at our Otuboi branch in the near future.
